About Our Craft

Each piece is meticulously handcrafted by Irvan Aumeer, blending the raw beauty of cement with functional design. Our signature product is a dual-purpose masterpiece: initially serving as an elegant candle holder, then transforming into a sustainable flower pot or decorative artifact.

Using locally sourced materials and traditional techniques, we create durable pieces that tell a story of craftsmanship and sustainability. Each item carries the unique texture and character that only handmade cement can provide.

Based in the heart of Mauritius, our workshop is inspired by the island's natural beauty, which reflects in every piece we create.
